About

I love learning! This remains the strongest thread connecting the various endeavors and happenings of the past decade and a half.

My academic background combines Computer Science and Mathematics from McGill University with recent work in Machine Learning and Intelligent Systems at the Technical University of Berlin. I'm currently pursuing a Masters in Human Centered Artificial Intelligence at Technical University of Dublin (Expected 2026).

Teaching Philosophy

My teaching is informed by progressive education movements in both European and American contexts, heavily influenced by the praxis-oriented methods of Paulo Freire. Core principles include:

  • Student-centered learning: Empowering students to take ownership of their learning journey
  • Real-world application: Connecting theoretical concepts to practical projects
  • Critical thinking: Encouraging students to question, analyze, and construct knowledge
  • Collaborative learning: Building communities of practice
