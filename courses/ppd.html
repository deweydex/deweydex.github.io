Welcome to PPD!

My name is Josh—together we will embark on an adventure in learning a bit more about ourselves, understanding our goals and how to achieve them.

If you have had a Personal and Professional development class in the past, you might find that my interpretation is a bit different than your prior experiences.

What We Will (Broadly) Cover:

  • Processes for understanding what your goals are and how you can best achieve them
  • How systems and structures work and change (and how you relate to that change)
  • How to translate your goals into a series of steps and projects
  • How to gauge whether you have learned something sufficiently to move forward
  • Investigate and use a variety of tools to help you in this and other classes

How You Will Be Assessed This Year:

  • 50% Skills Demonstrations: in class
  • 50% Portfolio/Collection of Work: in and outside of class with 1:1 feedback

Philosophy

This course is about more than just getting a job—it's about understanding yourself, your goals, and how to navigate professional systems effectively. We'll use practical tools and exercises to help you develop both the hard skills (CV writing, coding profiles) and soft skills (self-awareness, goal-setting) needed for long-term career success.
