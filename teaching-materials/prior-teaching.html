Foundations of AI for Business

Exploring AI/ML concepts, tools, and best practices for business applications. This course covered LLM tools, prompt engineering, and LinkedIn Learning integration.

Course Overview

Over the course of this year, we explored a series of concepts, tools and best practices regarding three different forms of what generally is called "Artificial Intelligence" but we referred to as three distinct machine learning applications.

Key Topics

  • Week 1: Introduction - Who am I and Who are You?
  • Week 6-8: Prompt guides and multiline prompts
  • Week 9: Pair Querying Activity
  • LinkedIn Learning modules integration
  • Dispelling myths and addressing biases in AI
  • Ethical and safe use of ML systems

Skills Developed

  • Prompt engineering for LLMs (Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT, Deepseek, Perplexity)
  • Context, Format, and Tone in prompting
  • Cover letter and professional statement creation using AI
  • Critical evaluation of AI/ML systems
