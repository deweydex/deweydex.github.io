My Teaching Philosophy Resources

My teaching and content styles are informed by progressive education movements in both a European and American context. I owe a great debt to:

  • The praxis-oriented methods of Paulo Freire
  • Ideas around learner independence and self determination as described by Maria Montessori and John Dewey
  • More modern educational giants such as the creators of Big Picture School (Dennis Littky and Elliot Washor)
  • The late but great Dr. Ken Robinson

For Further Exploration

The best resources are often the ones you discover through your own exploration. When evaluating a resource, ask yourself:

  • Is this at the right level for me?
  • Does the teaching style work for me?
  • Is the information current and accurate?
  • Are there opportunities for practice?
  • Is there a community or support system?

If you find a resource that works well for you, please share it! Learning is better when we learn together.
